About

Biospectra 2015, the third edition of BMEA's inter-college technical symposium, will be taking a new dimension. It is going to have events, both technical as well as non-technical, catering various disciplines. Workshops on current biomedical areas of importance like advanced microprocessors for biomedical applications, will adore its crown this time. It is also going to witness camps and debates concerning the importance of Biomedical Engineering . Student interactive guest lectures are going to be a part of Biospectra 2015.

Biomedical engineering has been evolving at a rapid rate due to the various innovations and remarkable research that it has been witnessing in the recent past. As a multidisciplinary field, it is open to all sectors of science and thus it has bridged the gap between medicine and engineering.Bio-Medical Engineer's Association (BMEA), ever since its inception in 2012, has been working towards this.BMEA has organized 'Medxtreme', BMEA's intra-college technical symposium. Following its success, BMEA is looking forward to Biospectra'15.!
